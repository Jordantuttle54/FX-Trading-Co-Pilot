Paper Mode Notice

⚠ LIVE TRADING IS LOCKED
This system runs in paper-trading mode only. It may use live OANDA practice market data, but it does not place real-money trades.

Do not consider live trading until:
  • Minimum 6 months of forward testing is complete
  • The journal is complete and auditable
  • Positive expectancy is confirmed after realistic spreads
  • Drawdown is controlled and within limits
  • The AI has not breached any hard risk rules
